Problem

Mezo is planning a DEX-first launch, making concentrated liquidity a core primitive of its DeFi ecosystem. Uniswap V3-style concentrated liquidity positions require active management — when the price of an asset moves outside a liquidity provider’s selected range, the position stops earning fees entirely and sits idle.

Most retail LPs lack the time, tools, or expertise to monitor their positions 24/7 and manually rebalance when price drifts. This results in significant fee revenue being left on the table and discourages participation in liquidity provision on Mezo’s DEX.

Projects like UniRange (ETHGlobal) have demonstrated that automated rebalancing vaults can solve this problem by monitoring every swap and auto-rebalancing when price drifts out of range, keeping LPs earning fees 24/7. A project like this is a natural fit for Mezo’s DEX ecosystem and could become a core DeFi primitive on the network.

---

Proposed Solution

Build an automated LP rebalancing vault for Mezo’s DEX (Uniswap V3-compatible) that monitors concentrated liquidity positions and automatically rebalances them when price moves out of range. The vault should be inspired by projects like UniRange and adapted for Mezo’s Bitcoin-native DeFi ecosystem.

The core functionality should include:

* Automated rebalancing: Monitor on-chain swaps and price movements, automatically withdraw and re-deposit liquidity into a new optimal range when price drifts out of bounds.
* Vault deposits: Allow users to deposit tokens into a managed vault that handles LP position creation and management on their behalf.
* Range strategy configuration: Support configurable range width strategies (tight, medium, wide) so users can choose their risk/reward profile.
* Fee compounding: Automatically collect and reinvest earned trading fees back into the LP position to maximize returns.
* Position dashboard: Display real-time vault performance, current range, fee earnings, and rebalancing history for depositors.

Bonus Features:

Multi-pool vault strategies (e.g., BTC/MUSD + MEZO/MUSD)

Impermanent loss tracking and reporting

Historical APY analytics and backtesting tools

Technical Requirements

Monetization and Fees

The vault builder is encouraged to implement a sustainable business model. The smart contracts should support a configurable performance fee (e.g., percentage of earned fees) and/or a management fee on deposited assets. Builders retain full ownership of their fee structure.

Maintenance Commitment

Mezo is looking for long-term partners. Applicants must provide a written commitment to maintain, bug-fix, and upgrade the vault for at least 6 months post-deployment. Outline your planned support model in your submission.

Network

Mainnet and Testnet network details are available at: https://mezo.org/docs/users/getting-started/connect

Users can grab testnet BTC and MEZO from the faucet at: https://faucet.test.mezo.org/

Smart Contract Requirements

Uniswap V3 compatibility: Integrate with Uniswap V3 (or compatible fork) NonfungiblePositionManager and pool contracts on Mezo

Vault token (ERC-4626 or similar): Issue share tokens representing a depositor’s proportional ownership in the vault

Rebalancing trigger mechanism: Use Chainlink Automation, Gelato, or a custom keeper bot to trigger rebalances when price moves out of range

Slippage protection: Implement configurable slippage tolerance and MEV-resistant swap execution during rebalances

Emergency pause: Admin function to pause vault operations in case of exploit or extreme market conditions

Wallet Integration

Required wallet support: MetaMask and other EVM wallets supported on Mezo (Taho, Zerion, OKX Wallet)

Bonus wallet support: Support Bitcoin wallets with @mezo-org/passport (built on RainbowKit) — Unisat, OKX, Xverse

Clean, intuitive UI for depositing into and withdrawing from vaults

Display key vault metrics: current range, TVL, APY, rebalance count, fee earnings

Security

Since this vault manages user funds, the security review will focus on:

Fund safety — Ensuring deposited tokens cannot be drained or locked permanently

Rebalancing logic — Correct handling of token swaps, range calculations, and position management

Access controls — Proper permissioning for keeper/admin functions and emergency pause



Developers must:

Provide comprehensive test coverage for all smart contract functions

Remediate any critical or high-severity findings before final acceptance

Mainnet deployment and reward payment are contingent on passing security review
